Hey there! Welcome to VYoGa, developed in 2022 by PES UNIVERSITY final year students.

Our aim is to develop an interactive website that guides trainee with correct yoga posture without an instructor and acts as a companion while you perform yoga.

VYoGa is inspired by various machine learning models for visual recognition.We experimented by taking this as our final year project.

VYoGa uses mediapipe (developed by google) for pose estimation and Django(as website backend).
