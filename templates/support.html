VYoGa support

BASIC :

Make sure your system is connected to decent internet.
Make sure you're in a well lit up room with system camera facing you.
Make sure your whole body is visible on the screen.



COURSE :

Contains 3 consecutive poses that is to be performed one after another.
Along with your real time video, pose image will also to displayed seeing which you need to perform the pose for 1 minute without making any error.
If failed, timer will restart so you again need to perform the same pose for 1 minute.
After you're done with first pose next pose will be displayed and in the same way you need to perform 3rd pose to complete the course.



PRACTICE :

If you have problem with any pose and you want to learn how to correct yourself in any particular pose then PRACTICE section is here for your help.
